An exchange is a place for transactions between buyers and sellers, most often through the use of intermediaries. Such intermediaries represent on the stock exchange the interests of both producers of goods and owners of securities, as well as the interests of their buyers.

Exchange device

It is an organized market where the rules of perfect competition apply. Prices are formed solely under the influence of supply and demand and are published daily. Their fixation with subsequent publication is carried out at the beginning and at the end of the working day. All market participants are provided with the same conditions for making transactions and carrying out trading operations. All market participants are subject to the same rules that are established on the exchange.

The main feature of exchange trading

Exchange trading does not imply the physical presence at the time of the transaction of the subjects of trade - goods and securities. The presence in the warehouses of goods of the appropriate quality and in sufficient quantities, as well as securities in banks with the right of ownership to them, is confirmed by the relevant documents.

All participants in transactions have the advantages of trading on the stock exchange

  • Suppliers on the stock exchange profitably sell their goods
  • Buyers get free access to information about goods and prices, have the opportunity to take advantage of free competition between sellers
  • Exchange players receive commissions for completed transactions

Advantages and disadvantages of working in trading

Professional traders must have specialized education. The difference between a professional trader and a non-professional trader is that a professional trader is completely independent in making his decisions.

On the one hand, this is a greater risk, but on the other hand, the result of the activity depends solely on the professional trader himself.

Advantages

  • with good luck and professionalism, there is an opportunity to make a lot of money
  • independence (for a professional trader)
  • excitement from the constant game

Disadvantages

  • risk of loss
  • the attitude other people towards traders as unscrupulous persons and speculators
  • high emotional tension
